About

Hi, I’m Taranveer Singh Gill, a Data Science graduate with a strong foundation in analytics, machine learning, and real-world data applications, backed by an undergraduate background in Mechanical Engineering and hands-on industry experience.

I earned my Master’s in Data Science from the University at Buffalo (SUNY), where I developed practical expertise in data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning through applied coursework and projects.

During my bachelor’s at Vellore Institute of Technology, I represented my university at Baja SAE USA, where our team ranked 4th worldwide. This experience strengthened my problem-solving mindset, teamwork, and ability to deliver results under pressure—skills that continue to shape my approach to technical challenges.

My transition into data science began through a multidisciplinary project focused on designing a drone for algae detection and cleaning in water bodies, where we combined mechanical design with OpenCV-based image processing. Working at the intersection of engineering and data sparked my interest in extracting insights from complex systems.

Professionally, I worked as a Data Validation Engineer at Numeros Motors, where I analyzed large-scale testing and validation datasets, ensured system performance met specifications, and communicated insights through clear visualizations and reports to cross-functional stakeholders.

Currently, as a master’s student in Data Science, I have developed a strong command of data analysis, statistical modeling, and machine learning while balancing on-campus work responsibilities. I am now seeking data-focused roles where I can apply my technical skills, continue learning, and contribute to impactful, data-driven decision-making.
